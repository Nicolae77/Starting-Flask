What is Digital Network?


Two or more electronic devices connected together wired or wireless create a digital network.
Network is used for:
  • Share information and data.
  • Share resources such as faxes, printers, and services.
  • Remote communication.
The basic components of the network between two devices are:
  • Applications that want to communicate.
  • A common language (protocol)
  • A network interface (NIC)
  • A transmission media to physically connect them(coaxial cable, twisted-pair, satellite, fiber optic)
